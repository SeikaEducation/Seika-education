About Us

Seika Education Consultancy is dedicated to helping students achieve their dreams of studying in Japan.

Founded with a passion for education, we provide expert guidance on university applications, visa processes, and language training.

Message From The MD/CEO Of
Seika Education Consultancy

It is with great pleasure that I announce the establishment of Seika Education Consultancy. I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude to all the students, instructors, and staff members for their dedication and unwavering support.

In today’s globalized world, Seika Education Consultancy, located in Butwal-9, Milan Chowk, is committed to promoting the Japanese language and fostering an educational environment rooted in peace and cross-cultural understanding of Japan. Since its inception, Seika Education Consultancy has strived to be a high-quality and progressive Japanese language school, guided by an innovative teaching philosophy.

With a team of enthusiastic staff and professional instructors, we offer a vibrant atmosphere and a wide range of specialized, goal-oriented Japanese language and cultural programs. We encourage students to embrace new challenges and pursue their dreams. If you aspire to study abroad in Japan, we are dedicated to providing our utmost support to help you achieve your goals.

We warmly welcome you to join us, and we look forward to supporting you on your educational journey.
